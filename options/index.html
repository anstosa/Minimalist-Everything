      Credits

      My name is Ansel Santosa [@anstosa].

      I'm a student, UX Designer, and Front-end Web Developer. I solve problems, write code, follow tech news, and play games.

      I created Minimalist to enable people to take control of the way they experience their favorite websites. Inspired by the success of my four other Minimalist extensions, I wanted users to be able to customize any website, not just my personal favorites.
